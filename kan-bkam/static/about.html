About Kan Bkam

Kan Bkam is a price comparison platform that helps you find the best deals across multiple online stores.

Our mission is to save you time and money by comparing prices from various retailers in one place.

We currently track prices from Amazon, Tesco, Target, and more stores coming soon!

The platform was launched in 2025 and is constantly being improved with new features.
